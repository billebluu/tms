How To Use TM Simulator

Go To Features and hover the selected operation

First step! Go to Features menu. When you hover the operation card, you'll see "Single-tape" or "Multi-tape" button.

Choose the TM type & enter the input needed

Choose one type of TM according to your needs! After you entered the simulator page, enter the input needed based on the available input box(es)

Click the button

After you entered the input(s), Click "Initialize Tape". Then, you can click "Start" button, or if you want to see the transition one by one, you can click the "Next step" button
